\section{Atomic Motions}

Symbols stay prettified at all times.
Each symbol behaves as a single character for navigation and editing.

\subsection{Navigation}

Use "h" and "l" to move through symbols one by one:
$\alpha + \beta = \gamma$

Use "w", "b", "e" to jump between words and symbols:
$\sum_{i=0}^{n} \alpha_i \to \infty$

\subsection{Editing}

Press "x" on a symbol to delete it entirely:
$f(\alpha, \beta) \leq \gamma + \delta$

Press "s" on a symbol to substitute it:
$\lambda \Rightarrow \sigma$

Press "cw" on a symbol to change the word:
$\forall x \in \mathbb{R}, \exists \epsilon > 0$

Enter insert mode and press Backspace after a symbol:
$\nabla \times \Phi = \partial \Psi$
